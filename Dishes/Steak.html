Steak

Description

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Mouth-watering steak better than grilled! This recipe for cast iron seared steak will have you forgetting about the grill.

Ingredients

  • 2x striplion steaks
  • 2x tablespoons of butter
  • salt and black pepper
  • Optional:2x fresh thyme sprigs

Steps

  1. Let steaks stand at room temperature for 30 min.
  2. Season steaks on both sides with a generous amount of salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil coated cast iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook steaks for 2-3 minutes per side (or until seared).
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low and add butter and thyme.
  6. Cook for 2 more minutes, basting steaks continually.
  7. Let steaks rest for 10 mins prior to eating.
